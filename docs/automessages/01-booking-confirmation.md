# 1. Booking confirmation + guest-count ask

Send: immediately on booking confirmation. All homes.

## Current version (guest-portal era) — recommended

```
Hi {{guest_first_name}},

Thanks so much for booking our home! We're honored you picked us and excited to host your lakeside retreat from {{check_in_date}} through {{check_out_date}}.

Please register through our guest portal link below. You may update your details later if anything changes at the same link.

————GUEST PORTAL————

https://guest.summitlakeside.com

———^^CLICK ABOVE^^———

Once registered, you'll have access to everything you need for your stay: check-in instructions, driving directions, door code, parking info, and the full house manual (wifi, temperature control, hot tub, sauna, etc.).

Our standard check in time is {{check_in_time}}, and checkout is at {{check_out_time}}.

Any add-ons like early or late checkout, firewood delivery, and other services can be booked and paid for directly in our guest portal.

If questions come up at any point, feel free to reach out.

In the meantime, could you please confirm the number of adults, children, and pets in your group?

Thanks,
Feliks
```
