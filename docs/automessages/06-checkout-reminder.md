# 6. Checkout reminder

Send: evening before checkout. All homes.

## Current version (with $25/hr late-checkout upsell) — recommended

```
Hi {{guest_first_name}},

We hope you've been having a great time at the lakehouse! Thank you so much for staying with us.

Your check out time tomorrow is at {{check_out_time}}.

Late checkout can be booked and paid directly in our guest portal under Add-Ons (guest.summitlakeside.com) — $25/hr, until 12pm or 1pm, subject to availability. Please contact us if you have an issue; leaving after check out without prior notice will incur a $50 fee.

Before you head out, please be sure to:

1. Remove all personal belongings. Items left behind can be shipped back to you if you provide a pre-paid shipping label. A $50 processing fee will be applied for all shipments.

2. Remove all trash. Please be aware that the cleaning fee is for basic whole house cleaning only. Excessive dirt, stains, and garbage left behind will incur additional cleaning fees.

If the trash bins outside are full, you can place bags next to them and we will handle it from there.

3. Report any damages caused, or if you received a citation from security. Unreported citations will incur an additional $25 processing fee.

All bedding, linens, and towels you can leave as they are on beds or floors and we will take care of it from there.

Thank you again for staying with us, and we hope to host you again soon. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out.

Thanks,
Feliks
Summit Lakeside Rentals
```

## Variant without the late-checkout offer

Use when there's a same-day check-in (drop the upsell paragraph from the version above).
